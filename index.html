Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about DevConf 2026? We've got answers. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our team.

When and where will DevConf 2026 take place? +

DevConf 2026 will be held over three days at the Grand Convention Center. Exact dates and venue directions will be shared in your confirmation email after registration.

Will sessions be recorded and available later? +

Yes! All keynote sessions and most breakout talks will be recorded and made available to registered attendees within a week after the conference.

Is there a student discount available? +

Absolutely. Students with a valid ID can avail up to 50% off on all ticket tiers. Apply the discount code at checkout after verification.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +

Full refunds are available up to 15 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another attendee free of charge.

What safety measures are in place at the venue? +

The venue follows standard health and safety protocols including on-site medical staff, clearly marked emergency exits, and trained volunteers throughout the event.
